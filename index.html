How it works:

  1. Click Start Recording.
  2. Allow Microphone: Grant permission to record your voice.
  3. Select Tab: Choose your Google Meet or YouTube tab.
  4. CRITICAL: Check the "Share tab audio" toggle in the bottom left of the selection window.
  5. Click Share to start capturing both sources.
